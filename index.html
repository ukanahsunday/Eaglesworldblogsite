Why Eaglesworld Global Schools?

Nursery Schools' Curriculum

We operate the Montessori method which support pupils' learning process in all curriculum areas.

Primary Schools' Curriculum

A blend of Nigerian and other international curricula and is carefully aligned with the International Primary Curriculum (IPC).

Secondary Schools' Curriculum

A blend of Nigerian and British curricula to equip students for entry to top advanced level undergraduate programmes across the world.

World Class School

Eaglesworld Global School is the only Secondary School in Africa to be Accredited by NEASC with highest standards that are globally recognised.

ACCA

Only Secondary school students in Nigeria to pass the foundation level of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountant (ACCA)..

British Council Award

Eaglesworld schools won Community Initiative Award 2019 for its student-led water borehole initiative.

The focus of the Eaglesworld Global Schools’ Trust Council is the development of world-class schools. World-class in all facets of our operations – curriculum, teaching methodologies, staff quality, libraries, technology, infrastructure, and so on.

It is our vision and goal that the students can contribute to this mission by having: the skills and qualifications to compete with, and most importantly work with the best; with an irrepressible mind that allows the child to thrive under pressure and by inculcating high moral and ethical values that will prepare them for a life of service and commitment.
